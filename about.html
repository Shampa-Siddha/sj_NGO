ABOUT US

The dedicated Social Organization purely working for the development of our society through various Activities and Programmes also provide different Services to the people; specially for underprivileged section for the last 50 years with the Ideology & guideline of Sri Ajay Kumar Das, our beloved Founder.
Our mission is to empower individuals and communities through education, cultural preservation, sports, and social welfare initiatives. We strive to create a harmonious society by promoting values of humanity, inclusivity, and service to the underprivileged, fostering a brighter and more equitable future for all.
Our vision is to build a compassionate and inclusive society where every individual has the opportunity to thrive, enriched by cultural heritage, supported by social welfare, and united by the spirit of humanity and service.
